About

Leadership Built Through Action

A dedicated leader in youth empowerment and political activism, T P Asharafali has served in multiple capacities to advocate for rights and opportunities for young people. From grassroots governance in local panchayats to national youth leadership, his work focuses on creating a more inclusive and just society while pursuing doctoral research at Jamal Mohamed College, Trichy.
